SARAN ELECTRICALS

Saran Electric, based in Kolkata GPO, is a reputable electrical company that has been serving the community for the past 32 years. Specializing in the sale of bulbs, fans, and a wide range of other electronic appliances, Saran Electric has established itself as a trusted name in the market. With a long-standing commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, the company continues to thrive and maintain a strong presence in the industry, catering to the needs of both individual consumers and businesses with reliable products and exceptional service.
